About us

We at Chess Club IIT Kanpur conduct various tournaments and sessions for the people at our campus and other
. Our major initiatives include Chess in Slums, where we visit remote areas and introduce the beautiful game to
underprivileged children, and a FIDE-styled IITK Chess Championship Cycle comprising of events like Grand Swiss, Grand Prix, a Candidates Tournament and an IITK Chess Championship Match.
